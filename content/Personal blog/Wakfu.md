# Stats

Intelligence
- 10 resist -> HP; elemental resist is SUPER importance
Strength
- mastery that suits most used spells; be it AOE, single, melee
Agility
- depends on role; need to buff first? need to lock or stay mobile ?
Chance
- often % crit or block
Major
- always AP
